About

E-Summit'21

A strong, unshakeable passion to pursue anything is required for a successful future. E-SUMMIT’21 provides a path for the same, by bringing together masters of the business world, exciting competitions and informal nights.

E-SUMMIT is a stage shared by experts that mastered their art in corporate world and young minds hungry to drive out changes in the world.

Events

Our Events

Change Makers

Masters of their own spells in the business world share their stories, perspectives, and lessons providing clarity to the young minds.

Ideathon

Imbue the spirit of entrepreneurship as you Ideate and build solutions to real-world problems in view of future startup prospectives.

Creators Table

Discussion tables where people with similar interests talk and deliberate on issues and themes that pique their attention in an open forum.

Design-Develop

Participants demonstrate their designing skills, unleashing their creativity as they design develop and deliver over previously shared problem statements.

Marketing Mockup

Spark your creative genius with this event, where the contestants need to market a practically useless product featuring their advertisement posters.

Dalal Street Quest

A simulated investment prospect where participants invest and learn to manage their assets and resources in the process to make a fortune.

Executive Symposium

Participants experience different roles in a firm's boardroom and deliberate over real-life situations and drive out solutions for them.

Informal/ Gaming Night

Just when you thought it's over, we have something exciting for all. Sit back and enjoy as we gather together to be entertained!

FAQ'S

Frequently Asked Questions

What is E-summit all about?

E-Summit, a 3 day online event organized by E-cell of JUIT, features groundbreaking events and competitions including Ideathon, Entrepreneurship talks, Boardroom meetings, Design and Marketing events and much more to take you on an enthralling and amazing learning experience.

Can I form teams with students of other colleges?

Yes, it is an open event. You're free to participate with your friends from other colleges.

Is it compulsory to take part in all of the events?

No, once you have paid the fees, you are eligible to take part in all the events, but no compulsion exists.

Who all can be a part of the E-summit?

Any college student, regardless of their major or college is eligible to be a part of the summit.

Can I remain updated about the event?

For regular updates follow us on instagram @tiedcjuit.
As for those who have registered, will be receiving the updates via mail.
